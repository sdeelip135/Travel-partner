Coorg

Day 1

Time: Morning

Activity: Mercara Gold Estate Coffee Plantation

Tips: Mercara Gold Estate is a 25 year old coffee and spice farm 8 KM from Madikeri, Coorg.

Amount: Entry fee ₹200 per person

Day 2

Time: Afternoon

Activity: Mandalpatti Peak

Tips: Mandalpatti, crowned at a height of 1600m, is situated at around 18 km from Madikeri,Coorg.

Amount: No entry fee

Day 3

Time: Evening

Activity: Raja's Seat

Tips: There is a 15 minute musical fountain show every day which starts at 7 pm on weekdays and 7.15 pm on weekends..

Amount: Entry fee ₹50 per person
