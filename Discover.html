Discover Delhi and Mysore

Delhi

Delhi, the capital city of India, is known for its rich history and vibrant culture. It has numerous historic monuments like the Red Fort, Qutub Minar, and India Gate, along with bustling markets and delicious street food.

Mysore

Mysore, located in the southern state of Karnataka, is famous for its royal heritage, intricate architecture, and the majestic Mysore Palace. It is also known for its silk sarees, sandalwood products, and the vibrant Dasara festival.
