Enroute of Visiting Jammu & Kashmir

Jammu and Kashmir

Jammu and Kashmir is a region administered by India as a union territory and consists of three regions: Jammu, the Kashmir Valley, and Ladakh.

Key places to visit:

  • Srinagar
  • Gulmarg
  • Pahalgam
  • Sonamarg
  • Leh
  • Ladakh

Places to Visit in Jammu and Kashmir

Srinagar

Srinagar is known for its beautiful gardens, houseboats, and the famous Dal Lake.

Top attractions:

  • Dal Lake
  • Shalimar Bagh
  • Nishat Bagh
  • Shankaracharya Temple

Gulmarg

Gulmarg is a popular skiing destination and offers breathtaking views of snow-capped mountains.

Top attractions:

  • Gulmarg Gondola
  • Gulmarg Golf Course
  • Alpather Lake

Pahalgam

Pahalgam is known for its lush green valleys and is a popular base for trekking.

Top attractions:

  • Betaab Valley
  • Aru Valley
  • Lidder River

Leh

Leh is the capital of Ladakh and is known for its Buddhist monasteries and stunning landscapes.

Top attractions:

  • Thiksey Monastery
  • Hemis Monastery
  • Pangong Lake

Ladakh

Ladakh offers mesmerizing landscapes, high-altitude lakes, and adventurous trekking routes.

Top attractions:

  • Nubra Valley
  • Tso Moriri Lake
  • Khardung La Pass
